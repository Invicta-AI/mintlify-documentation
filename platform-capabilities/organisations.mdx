## What is an Organization?

<Info>
  This feature is available exclusively for Business and Enterprise customers.
</Info>

An Organization is a unique environment on Invicta AI that enables users to:

1. Create workspaces for sharing and collaborating on agents with organization members.
2. Access a shared pool of Compute Points for all organization users.
3. Allow organization owners to analyze Invicta AI activity (coming soon).

## Accessing Organizations

To gain access to the Organization feature:

1. Obtain a Business Subscription
2. Contact us for an Enterprise Subscription

<Card title="Get Enterprise Access" icon="building" href="#">
  Reach out to our sales team to discuss Enterprise Subscription options.
</Card>

## Organization Capacity

1. Business Subscription: Maximum 3 members

2. Enterprise Subscription: 2 or more members (customizable)

## Managing Your Organization

To manage your organization:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Organization Settings">
    Click on the profile menu in the bottom left corner and select
    "Organization".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Organization">
    In the Organization settings, you can: - Invite other members via email -
    View your pool of compute points - Rename your organization
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Adding New Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate Invitation">Click the "Add Members" button.</Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email Addresses">
    Input the email addresses of the people you want to invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invitations">
    Send the invitations. Recipients will receive an email invitation valid for
    7 days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Invitees must already be registered on Invicta AI with the email addresses
  used for invitation.
</Warning>

## Workspaces

Workspaces allow you to share agents with specific members of your organization.

### Managing Workspaces

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Workspace Management">
    Open the profile menu in the bottom left and navigate to "Manage
    Workspaces".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and Manage Workspaces">
    Create new workspaces and manage existing ones by inviting specific
    organization members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Workspaces">
    Access your workspaces through the "Workspaces" page in the profile menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Adding Agents to Workspaces

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Agent Builder">Navigate to the Agent's Builder.</Step>
  <Step title="Set Visibility">Set the agent's visibility to "Workspace".</Step>
  <Step title="Select Workspace">
    Choose the workspace to add the agent to using the selector that appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Save the agent. It will now be visible to other users in the selected
    workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Users can view shared agents in the "Workspaces" page accessible through the
  profile menu.
</Tip>

## Business or Enterprise Compute Points

For Business or Enterprise customers, your company receives a pool of monthly compute points (CPs). All users added to the organization will consume CPs from this shared pool.

<Info>
  You can view your organization's CP balance on the "Organization" page.
</Info>

### CP Consumption Priority

If a user has both organizational and personal CP allocations:

1. Organizational CPs will be consumed first.
2. Personal CPs will only be used if organizational CPs are depleted.
